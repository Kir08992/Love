Mahal kong Kovivi

In this year's velvet night, my heart a trembling star,

I counted constellations, each a distant, hopeful scar.

Then, a light, a lunar glow, eclipsed the fading dawn,

And in that moonlit grace, my loneliness was gone.

A silver symphony, your presence filled the air,

Each whisper of your laughter, a balm for my despair.

You are the moon, a beacon in the darkest, coldest night,

And I, a yearning satellite, forever drawn to your light.

I long to be a star, a jewel in your celestial crown,

To dance with you in starlight, where shadows never drown.

Without you, my heart is a shooting star, lost in the void,

Yearning for the solace that your presence has deployed.

You are my compass, guiding me through life's uncertain maze,

Leading me to a haven where my soul finds its amaze.

A perfect haven, bathed in your lunar, gentle light,

Where I find solace, hope, and love, forever shining bright.
